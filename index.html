About Stellar Chaos

I'm Pinja and I'm a lifelong dog person, and I've almost always had a dog. I live with my husband and two sons in Oulu, Finland. Our family thrives on the companionship of dogs. Our four borzois and two rough collies keep our lives lively and full of fur. I believe in responsible breeding and prioritize the health and happiness of our dogs above all else. I have not bred any litters yet, but with careful planning there might be one in the future. My breeding plans are solely for borzois, I am not planning on any collie litters.

While I enjoy showing our dogs and participating in sighthound events (lure coursing, track racing), my main focus is creating a loving home where our furry friends can relax and be themselves.

The name Stellar Chaos comes from the essence of the borzoi. These dogs are incredibly beautiful, stellar, but they are also full of chaos and will bring much fun and chaos to their owners' lives. A borzoi owner will need huge amounts of humour to live with these creatures.
